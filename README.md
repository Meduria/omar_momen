<div id="about" class="about">

      <p><i class='far fa-dot-circle' style='font-size:7px'></i>
Since I was a child, I’ve always been curious about how the world works. I spent hours drawing, building small wooden models when I was twelve, and exploring game engines just to create anything that came to mind. I was also the kid who watched National Geographic endlessly, fascinated by nature, science, and anything new I could discover.</p>
<p><i class='far fa-dot-circle' style='font-size:7px'></i>
That curiosity pushed me to try many things as I grew up. I explored different sports like ping pong, karate, football, and handball. But the one sport that truly stayed with me was swimming. Being in the water makes me feel free, relaxed, and completely at peace .. it’s the activity where I truly felt I could push myself the most.</p>

<p><i class='far fa-dot-circle' style='font-size:7px'></i>
My interest in technology began early as well, but it took a real turn in 9th grade. We had a small programming lesson in school.. nothing big, nothing special.. and most students didn’t pay any attention to it. But for me, it was the moment everything changed. I became fascinated with the idea of building something from nothing, and that simple lesson became the spark that started my journey into web development.</p>

<p><i class='far fa-dot-circle' style='font-size:7px'></i>
From there, I continued learning on my own, spending hours experimenting, searching, and slowly improving. Today, I’ve been developing websites for about three years, using Visual Studio Code as my main tool and constantly learning new ways to bring ideas to life.</p>

<p><i class='far fa-dot-circle' style='font-size:7px'></i>
Alongside coding, I also explored design. About four years ago, I started creating social media designs and logos using Photoshop and Illustrator. Even though design is a skill I value, web development is where I feel most connected and where I truly express myself.</p>

<p><i class='far fa-dot-circle' style='font-size:7px'></i>
Now, I work as a freelancer, combining everything I’ve learned curiosity, creativity, problem-solving, and constant self-improvement. I aim to create a friendly, comfortable experience for anyone who works with me, while still delivering professional and thoughtful results.</p>

<p><i class='far fa-dot-circle' style='font-size:7px'></i>
If there’s one thing I hope people understand about me, it’s that my journey didn’t start with a plan. it started with curiosity, passion, and a genuine love for learning and creating.</p>
